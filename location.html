The ideal location for your holiday

Το ιδανικό σημείο για τις διακοπές σας

The modern, newly-built Sand and Sea Villas are along the beach, west of Chania, in Pyrgos Psilonerou, Platanias.

The Sand and Sea Villas are located in the area of Pyrgos Psilonerou, which is a 15 to 20 minutes drive west of the city of Chania.

There is easy access to the local bus, a supermarket, cafes, restaurants and bars. Maleme is just over 10 minutes walking distance and has cafes, taverns, bars, supermarket and fully-organised beaches. Nightlife The area has , makes it easy to bask in the well-organized tourist facilities, as well as the lively nightlife of Platanias, one of the most popular resort destinations in Crete, while at the same time being able to provide the tranquillity and serenity that can only be found in West Crete and in a peaceful villa, like the ones at Daphnis Villas.

The historical city of Chania is in a distance of 15 km, and you can get there by car, by bus or by taxi. You can use Daphnis Villas as a base for visiting the major attractions in Crete. Its location, close to Chania airport and port, and a stone’s throw away from the national road in Crete that leads to the cities of Chania and Heraklio, is ideal to do exactly that.

Distances: 200 m from the beach 15 km from the city of Chania 35 km from Chania Airport (CHQ) 23 km from Souda Port 33 km from Falassarna 57 km from Elafonisi 30 km from Kastelli, Kissamos 75 km from the city of Rethymno 153 km from the city of Heraklio

Put on your bathers and stroll down to the beach, lie on a sunbed and enjoy the Greek sunshine, delicious food, tasty drinks and good music.
