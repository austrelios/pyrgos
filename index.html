Extraordinary spaces inside & out. Crete's most private and exclusive luxury accommodation experiences in spectacular landscapes.

Set on a 2,800 sq. m peaceful private land surrounded by the nature, Villa Anastasia is a 520 sq. m luxury villa with a high standard construction quality and rich facilities. Facing towards the Cretan sea and only 3 km away from the sandy beach and the well known tourist area of Platanias, the villa offers unspoiled views to the sea and surrounding mountains. A peaceful atmosphere and modern amenities for restful holidays, perfect for families and children, couples, newly-weds and people with good taste. Enjoy tranquillity, comfort and luxuriousness in this exquisite and unique villa.
