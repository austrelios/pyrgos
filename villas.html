Discover the Sea Waves Villas

Ανακάλυψε τις βίλες Sea Waves

Discover seaside luxury in our newly built holiday villas located by the beach. Wake up to the sounds and smells of the sea. Savour sunsets sipping a cocktail by your private pool.

The perfect getaway for romantic couples, families and groups of friends.

Villa 1

This two level fully air-conditioned villa has the lounge, kitchen and dining areas on the ground floor. The lounge and dining area open up to the back courtyard. The ground floor bedroom has two twin beds and opens up directly to the courtyard.

There is a driveway at the front of the villa that fits two cars.

The first floor has two bedrooms and a bathroom. The master bedroom has a queen bed and has its own bathroom. The second bedroom has two twin beds and a balcony. A bathroom caters to the guests of the second bathroom and rooftop. A large balcony wraps around the first floor.

Relax on the roof by the private swimming pool and enjoy views of the sea and mountains. The pool is furnished with lounge chairs and side tables and hose for showering after your swim.

Villa 2

This three level villa has the lounge, kitchen and dining areas on the ground floor. The lounge and dining area open up to the back courtyard. The bedroom on this level opens up directly to the courtyard.

There is space at the front of the villa that parks two cars.

There basement has a comforable lounge area as well as a the laundry room and a bathroom.

The first floor has two bedrooms and a bathroom. The master bedroom is has a queen bed and has its own bathroom. A bathroom caters to the guests of the second bathroom and rooftop. A large balcony wraps around the first floor.

Relax on the roof by the private swimming pool and enjoy views of the sea and mountains. The pool is furnished with lounge chairs and side tables and hose for showering after your swim.

Villa 3

The open floor plan kitchen, dining and lounge areas ensure a comfortable stay. The villa has three bedrooms with either double or twin beds. The villa comfortable fits six people.

The master bedroom has a queen bed and has its own bathroom. The villa has uninterrupted views of the sea which you can enjoy from relaxing by the ground floor swimming pool.

There is a ground level driveway for parking your car.

Description

Περιγραφή

The villas are located 15km west of Chania, Crete.
The villas are ideal for ideal for couples holidays, big families, and groups of friends.
The villas consist of three seafront villas, each with their own swimming pool.
The villas are located just 200 m from an exquisite NATURA-protected beach with rare flora and fauna and crystal-clear water. During the summer months, the Caretta-Caretta turtles are spawning.
The villas are brand new with a completion date of December 2023.
