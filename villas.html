Περιγραφή

The villas are located 15km west of Chania, Crete.
The villas are ideal for ideal for couples holidays, big families, and groups of friends.
The villas consist of three seafront villas, each with their own swimming pool.
The villas are located just 200 m from an exquisite NATURA-protected beach with rare flora and fauna and crystal-clear water. During the summer months, the Caretta-Caretta turtles are spawning.
The villas are brand new with a completion date of December 2023.
